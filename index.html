Mingjie Li

I am currently a 2nd-year master student (expected graduation time: March 2024) in Computer Science Department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Quanshi Zhang. Prior to that, I receieved the Bachelor degree in CS (IEEE honor class) from SJTU.

My current research interest lies in Explainable AI. This topic mainly focuses on designing tools to open the black-box of deep neural networks. I believe XAI could boost AI research. I am also interested in various exciting AI applications.

🔥 I am actively seeking for full-time machine learning engineer opportunities starting in 2024. Please feel free to contact me if interested.
